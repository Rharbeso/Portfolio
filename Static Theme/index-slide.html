My Customized Webpage

Hi, my name is Ryan and I am a recent graduate of Hussian College in Philadelphia! I specialize in Front End Web Design and UI/UX. I also am experienced in marketing research and website wireframing/prototyping. Below you will find all the information about myself that you should know.

Competitive

Beingcompetitiveis a must in any working enviornment. Striving to not only be better than others, but to be the best YOU that you can be! Staying motivated each and every day is a challenge everyone deals with. I find that having an open mind, getting good sleep, and a having positive work relations with your colleagues are key.

Connection

Being connected with yourself and your co-workers is always overlooked. Knowing what your strengths and weaknesses are is important. Also, just as important, is knowing your co-workers attributes. It is nice to be able to know who to seek out for information or who you may be able to assist on certain work projects.

Health

Staying healthy maybe what I value the most in life. Before work, before play, before almost anything. Health is the most precious gift anyone can receive. I practice yoga, weight training, and various sports in order to stay in shape and stay sharp. As I mentioned before; getting a good nights sleep and eating clean is just as important as staying active.

Organized

Everyone knows organization is an important trait to aquire. Having information or paperwork ready upon request is good practice of being organized. Your boss and co-workers will also notice if someone is organized or not. Your work area should be "clutter free" and clean at all times if possible.

Modernism

Keeping up with modern software and understanding how programs continue to change is crucial. The technology field is constantly changing and new routines, practices, and programs are always taking the place of others. Staying up to date with your practice is necessary to keep a job, even if you have to do some "homework" on your own time.

Visionary

I always envision the end even while at the start. It is good to have a gameplan no manner what you are doing. I will always have a mock project done up before beginning the project itself. Even if it is a simple wireframe or prototype; I at least have an idea and starting the project will be quicker and easier.

Skills

Personal Skills and Experience

I have learned a lot in during my courses at Hussian. As a class we dove into various languages as well as group projects for communication purposes. I have become a much more Complete and Visual coder! All of the collaboration, personal work, and visual lessons have helped me in many ways.

HTML

Hypertext Markup Language. Using this language I can create the basic structure of a webpage. HTML is the "backbone" of any site.

CSS

Cascading Style Sheet. Using this language I can style the HTML and give it "personality". CSS includes colors, sizing, fonts, and location of HTML elements.

Bootstrap

Twitter Bootstrap allows the user to take premade elements to use or to further customize on their own. I can use a premade Bootstrap navigation bar and use my own CSS to style is how I wish.

Color Theory

The understanding of what colors work and why. Colors are very important. They can reflect on your audience or be used to draw the users attention.

UI/UX

Knowing who the target customers are, and how to make their experience with your product the most rewarding or ‘delightful’ it can be, is the responsibility of the UX Design team. A User Interface Designer makes technology easy and intuitive for people to use. User interface designers work on the areas where users directly interact with the product.

Team Collaberation

I consider one of my strongest attributes is my ability to work with others. Getting along with colleagues is always beneficial and comes in handy when doing research and the actual developement process.

Research

What can be a long process may also be the most imporant step for a designer. Sites are planned according to the researched material. It includes everything from creating personas to coming with a page's/app's color scheme.
